HHD-Ethiopic: Historical Handwritten OCR Dataset for Ethiopic

Authors: Anonymous-Author

  • Historical Handwritten OCR Dataset for Ethiopic Script recognition:

    This dataset comprises approximately 80,000 historical handwritten text-line images of the Ethiopic script, each paired with its corresponding Ground-Truth (GT). It provides extensive data for training and testing recognition models. Both the images and their corresponding labels are stored in numpy format, with the images stored in their original size, allowing for more precise analysis and comparison of different recognition methods. The ground-truth sequences have a maximum length of 46 characters, and the numpy files are zero-padded. Additionally, a text-file equivalent of the ground-truth is provided.
